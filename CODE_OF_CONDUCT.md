# Code of Conduct

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks, insults, or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban contributors for behavior that violates this code.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer.
